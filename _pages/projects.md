## Reading

I enjoy reading and listening to books, particularly biographies and history. Some memorable books, in no particular order, include:

4. [The Rise and Fall of the Third Reich](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/767171.The_Rise_and_Fall_of_the_Third_Reich) by William L. Shirer
